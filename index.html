Find your local government meetings

Enter a U.S. address and get a calendar (.ics) of nearby council, board, and commission meetings — with agendas where available.

Host or embed this

This page calls a small backend API (it proxies the U.S. Census geocoder and the Legistar civic API, which don't allow direct browser calls). Self-host with python3 server.py, or point this page at a deployed backend by adding ?api=https://your-host to the URL.

Embed it anywhere:
<iframe src="https://YOUR-HOST/?embed=1" width="100%" height="640" style="border:0"></iframe>
